Spaghetti Carbonara

Italian
Easy
30 mins

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 plump garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. Finely chop the pancetta, having first removed any rind.
  3. Beat the eggs in a medium bowl, season with a little freshly grated black pepper and set everything aside.
  4. Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add the spaghetti and when the water comes back to the boil, cook at a constant simmer, covered, for 10 minutes or until al dente (just cooked).
  5. Squash the garlic with the blade of a knife, just to bruise it.
  6. While the spaghetti is cooking, fry the pancetta with the garlic.
  7. Keep the heat hot enough for the pancetta to crisp up a little, but don’t overdo it.
  8. Remove from the heat and discard the garlic.
  9. Keep the pancetta warm.
  10. When the pasta is ready, lift it from the water with a pasta fork or tongs and put it in the frying pan with the pancetta.
  11. Mix most of the cheese in with the eggs, keeping a small handful back for sprinkling over later.
  12. Take the pan of spaghetti and pancetta off the heat.
  13. Quickly pour in the eggs and cheese.
  14. Using the tongs or a long fork, lift up the spaghetti so it mixes easily with the egg mixture, which thickens but doesn’t scramble, and everything is coated.
  15. Add extra pasta cooking water to keep it saucy (several tablespoons should do it).
  16. Season with a little salt, if needed.
  17. Use a long-pronged fork to twist the pasta on to the serving plate or bowl.
  18. Serve immediately with a little sprinkling of the remaining cheese and a grating of black pepper.

Vegan Thai Green Curry

Thai
Medium
45 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 200g tofu, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 100g green beans, trimmed
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute.
  4. Stir in the green curry paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the tofu, bell peppers, and green beans.
  7. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  8. Stir in the soy sauce and lime juice.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.
